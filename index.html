OUR SERVICES

What We Do

Complete excavation and grading solutions for residential and commercial projects throughout Durham Region.

Excavation

Complete site excavation for foundations, basements, and large-scale construction projects. Safe and efficient digging with modern equipment.

Grading

Precision land grading for proper drainage, leveling, and site preparation. Ensuring your property has the right slope and foundation.

Interlock Preparation

Expert base preparation for interlocking patios, driveways, and walkways. Proper excavation and compaction for lasting results.

Gravel Driveways

New driveway installation and restoration. From clearing and grading to gravel placement and compaction for durable, drainable surfaces.

Trucking & Hauling

Professional material transport and debris removal. Topsoil, fill, gravel, and aggregate delivery to your site when you need it.

Site Preparation

Complete lot clearing and preparation for construction or landscaping. Tree removal, stump grinding, and debris cleanup included.
